The story behind
the garden bar

In the heart of Linthwaite, between the flower beds and the fence, something rather special emerged. Not a shed with ambition — a full-blown destination.

What started as a question — "what if we built a pub in the garden?" — became one of West Yorkshire's most characterful venues. Every brick, every bottle, every dart thrown at that board is a testament to what happens when you combine a good idea with a healthy dose of stubbornness.

Welcome to The Rockford Arms. You're most welcome here.
